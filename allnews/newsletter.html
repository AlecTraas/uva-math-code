UVa Math Newsletter: Virginia Math Bulletin

We have started a newsletter for the Department, the Virginia Math Bulletin. We intend to produce a new edition every year. If you would like to be added to our mailing list to receive an electronic version of the newsletter, please write to us at math-help@virginia.edu.
