New developments in 3- and 4-manifold topology

June 5 - 7, 2023

An in-person workshop at Department of Mathematics, University of Virginia, Charlottesville, VA

Financial support (deadline: March 14, 2022)

It is confirmed that we will be able to provide financial support through an NSF Conference grant to some of the participants who wish to attend the workshop and possibly the Virginia Mathematics Lectures by Mikhail Khovanov. Junior researchers may request reimbursement of their travel and lodging expenses to attend the conference. The support will be primarily directed towards PhD students, early career researchers, and members of underrepresented groups.

To request financial support:

  • Register and indicate the financial support request in the registration form below. Answer the additional questions in the "NSF reporting" section.
  • If you are a PhD student, have your advisor send a short recommendation letter (1 page limit) to {% include person_info_email_only.html UVA_id="yq2dw" %}
The deadline to request financial support is March 14, 2022.

Registration form

If you plan to attend the workshop (regardless of requesting financial support), please fill the registration form below.
  • The registration is free
  • You do not need to register to attend just the Virginia Mathematics Lectures
