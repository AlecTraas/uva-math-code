Geometry/Topology Conferences

##### Upcoming: ###### Sectional Meeting of the AMS The UVa math department is pleased to be hosting the Spring 2020 Southeast Sectional Meeting of the American Mathematical Society, Marh 13-15, 2020. UVa faculty will be co-organizing a wide variety of special sessions, including * [Special Session on Celebrating Diversity in Mathematics](https://www.ams.org/meetings/sectional/2273_program_ss20.html#title) : co-organized by Sara Maloni. * [Special Session on Homotopy Theory](https://www.ams.org/meetings/sectional/2273_program_ss15.html#title): co-organized by Julie Bergner and Nick Kuhn. * [Special Session on Knots and Links in Low-Dimensional Topology](https://www.ams.org/meetings/sectional/2273_program_ss5.html#title): co-organized by Thomas Mark. * [Special Session on Trends in Teichmuller Theory](https://www.ams.org/meetings/sectional/2273_program_ss21.html#title): co-organized by Thomas Koberda and Sara Maloni.
